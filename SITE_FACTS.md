## FAQ Content

**Q: Does Justin accept sponsorships or free products?**
No. Every drink and every visit is paid for out of pocket. There are no sponsorships, partnerships, or free products of any kind. This is what makes the reviews trustworthy.

**Q: How often are reviews updated?**
Justin revisits shops quarterly to keep information current. Outdated hours, closed shops, and menu changes are corrected on an ongoing basis.

**Q: Does Justin visit every shop himself?**
Yes. Every shop reviewed on the site has been visited in person by Justin. All photos are original and taken during his visits.

**Q: What neighborhoods does the guide cover?**
Koreatown is the most thoroughly covered neighborhood with 30+ shops documented. San Gabriel Valley and Silver Lake guides are coming soon. The long-term goal is to document every boba shop in LA County.

**Q: How do you choose which shops to review?**
Justin reviews every boba shop he can find across LA County, not just the popular ones. The goal is completeness, not a curated best-of list.

**Q: Is there a way to contact Justin?**
Yes. Email: justin@justinsather.com. Instagram: @labobaguide.
